## Connector support levels

Not all connectors are the same. Airbyte uses four support tiers:

| Tier | Maintained by | Production ready | Support |
|------|--------------|------------------|---------|
| **Airbyte** | Airbyte team | Yes | SLA for Premium customers |
| **Enterprise** | Airbyte team | Yes | SLA for Premium customers |
| **Marketplace** | Community | No SLA | No support |
| **Custom** | You | Not guaranteed | You own it |

See the [Connector Support Levels](/connectors/connector-support-levels) page for full details on what each tier means for your production data pipelines.

## Sync modes

Every connector supports a subset of Airbyte's sync modes:

- **Full Refresh - Overwrite**: Replace all data in the destination with the latest source data.
- **Full Refresh - Append**: Add all source records to the destination on each sync without deleting existing records.
- **Incremental - Append**: Only sync new or updated records, appending them to the destination.
- **Incremental - Append + Deduped**: Sync new/updated records and deduplicate based on a primary key.

The sync modes available depend on both the source and destination connectors you choose.
